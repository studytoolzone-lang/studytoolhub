About BMI

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m² where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m² is their height in meters squared.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: Below 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 - 29.9
  • Obese: 30 and above

Limitations

BMI doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, overall body composition, and racial/sex differences. It's a screening tool, not a diagnostic of body fatness or health.
